The Zergling

The Zergling is a small and fast melee attacker and the backbone of the Zerg army. Individual Zerglings are weak, but large groups can surround and terrorize enemy ground forces. In such groups, they soak a lot of incoming damage and provide a shield for more expensive units.

A Zergling

Zergling in the campaign

Zerglings are Tier 1 units and can be spawned early. They require only a Spawning Pool. Zergling rushes are a threat for which opponents must prepare.

Upgrades

Zerglings have two upgrades:

  • Metabolic Boost greatly increases the Zerglings' speed.
  • Adrenal Glands quickens the Zerglings' attack speed.
